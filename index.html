About Me

 

My name is Rich Donovan. I grew up in Blaine, Minnesota. I attended Totino-Grace High School and graduated in 2012. I went on to the University of St. Thomas where I studied Marketing and played football. I graduated from the University of St. Thomas in 2016 with a Bachelor of Science in Marketing Management.

After college I found a job at a small consulting company called Profit Builder Network in Minneapolis. We help clients with general business practices. My role is to manage business projects and assist with our cleints' marketing needs.

I began computer programming in 2018 earning a certificate of completion from the University of Minnesota Coding Bootcamp. During the course we learned JavaScript, JQuery, AJAX, node.js, express.js, and handlebars. We also learned how to use both SQL and NoSQL databases. We utilized MySQL (SQL database) as well as Mongo DB (NoSQL Database). Take a look at some of my projects to see if I can meet your programming needs.
